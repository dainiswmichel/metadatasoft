Quote:

"Metadata acts as contextual agent, documenting the 'who, what, where, when, why, and how', and gives an image identity and authenticity. Once data about the image is removed much of its meaning is lost. This can lead to unfortunate consequences. Under an act recently passed in the UK these orphaned photos could be used by for-profit companies without the consent of the copyright holder(s)."
