"What most creators don't realize is that platforms don't just display your metadata—they completely reprocess it. We've measured up to 90% loss of embedded metadata during standard uploads to major services."

— Maria Pallante, former U.S. Register of Copyrights

Your Metadata Dies at the Gate

The reality is stark: when your content enters a platform's ecosystem, your original attribution data is typically replaced with whatever the platform decides to keep. This causes several critical problems:

  • Proprietary transforms strip critical fields like composer, publisher, and rights details
  • Platform-specific IDs replace your standard identifiers (ISRC, ISWC)
  • Formatting and compression discard "non-essential" attribution
  • No verification exists to ensure platform-displayed metadata matches your original
