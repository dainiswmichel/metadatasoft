✅ ISRC Sheet

What it is: An ISRC identifies each unique sound recording or music video.

Why it matters: ISRCs are required for streaming, digital distribution, and royalty tracking.

  • Lists all ISRCs for your recordings.
  • Exportable as CSV, JSON, XML for easy upload.

External Links:

  • IFPI ISRC Handbook
  • SoundExchange ISRC Guide

✅ DA1XML Export

What it is: A full metadata export in DA1XML format.

Why it matters: A single source of truth for metadata, legal compliance, and archival purposes.

  • Human-readable and machine-readable XML file.
  • Useful for audits and proof of ownership.

External Links:

  • W3C XML Overview
  • DDEX ERN Standards

✅ PRO Registration Sheet

What it is: Report for Performing Rights Organizations (PROs) registration.

Why it matters: Registers songs with PROs to ensure royalty payment.

  • Title
  • Writers / Publishers
  • ISWC
  • Splits and Shares

External Links:

  • ASCAP Registration

✅ Neighboring Rights Report

What it is: Report for neighboring rights royalty collection.

Why it matters: Royalties for recording performances internationally.

  • Performer Names and Roles

  • Master Owner (Label)
