✅ What is metaMetaData?

metaMetaData refers to metadata about metadata. It describes the structure, rules, and mappings that govern how different metadata standards interconnect and function together.

DA1metaMetaData is the industry’s first comprehensive, immutable, and structured metaMetaData system designed specifically for media files and digital content. It acts as the single source of truth, ensuring consistency and accuracy across every distribution platform and file format.

By utilizing DA1metaMetaData, content creators and rights holders can map a single authoritative metadata entry to multiple file formats and platforms—without duplication, loss, or human error.

✅ DA1metaMetaData Maps to 7 Major Filetypes

DA1metaMetaData bridges the gap between different file formats and their native metadata standards. By creating a unified data layer, DA1 ensures accurate mapping to the 7 most common audio and video filetypes:

  • WAV (BWF / INFO / iXML)
  • MP3 (ID3v2 / APEv2)
  • FLAC (Vorbis Comments)
  • AIFF (ID3v2 / iXML)
  • M4A (MP4 Metadata / iTunes Atoms)
  • OGG (Vorbis Comments)
  • MP4 (ISO BMFF / iTunes Atoms)

✅ Problems DA1metaMetaData Solves

Fragmented Metadata Systems: Metadata today is fragmented across filetypes and platforms. DA1metaMetaData creates a unified structure that maps to all major standards, eliminating duplication and inconsistencies.

Loss of Data Integrity: Metadata is often stripped, overwritten, or lost when files move between platforms. DA1metaMetaData ensures permanent, immutable provenance by embedding consistent information across all outputs.

Royalties and Rights Management: Incomplete or inaccurate metadata leads to lost royalties and disputes. DA1metaMetaData provides a verifiable, accurate source of truth that supports PRO registration, ISRC assignment, and neighboring rights reporting.

Compliance and Legal Proof: DA1metaMetaData produces machine-readable exports (DA1XML) that provide audit trails, ownership validation, and legal compliance documentation on demand.
