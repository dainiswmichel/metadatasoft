When Metadata Meets Blockchain: We Come in Peace

Dainis W. Michel March 29, 2025 6 min read

The Problem: Metadata Fragmentation Is Costing Everyone

"There is currently no single standard for what music metadata should include or how it should be formatted."

— Ellie Rennie, When Music Metadata Meets Blockchain Governance

The music industry faces a crisis of disconnection. As Ellie Rennie perfectly captured in her insightful analysis, metadata fragmentation has created a chaotic ecosystem where creators lose royalties, platforms bear legal risks, and the entire value chain suffers from inefficiency and mistrust.

When a single piece of music travels from creator to consumer today, its identity becomes fragmented across at least seven different file formats (WAV, MP3, FLAC, AIFF, M4A, OGG, MP4), each with their own conflicting metadata standards. The result?

  • Artists and songwriters miss payments when their names get stripped from files
  • Platforms face legal exposure from using works with unclear attribution
  • Researchers struggle to trace lineage as metadata gets corrupted in transfer
  • Collection societies waste resources reconciling contradictory data

We're not here to compete. We're here to connect.

The DA1 Approach: A Bridge, Not a Wall

DA1 was built with a simple philosophy: standards should unite, not divide. Our metaMetaData system doesn't replace existing workflows—it connects them.

We've created a unified metadata layer that:

  1. Maps seamlessly across all 7 major audio and video file formats
  2. Preserves data integrity through blockchain verification
  3. Ensures attribution follows the content wherever it travels
  4. Simplifies compliance with transparent audit trails

Our approach isn't revolutionary—it's evolutionary. We're not asking anyone to abandon their existing systems. We're offering a translation layer that makes them all work better together.

The Solution: DA1metaMetaData

The DA1metaMetaData system creates a permanent bridge between all major metadata standards:

  • WAV (BWF / INFO / iXML)
  • MP3 (ID3v2 / APEv2)
  • FLAC (Vorbis Comments)
  • AIFF (ID3v2 / iXML)
  • M4A (MP4 Metadata / iTunes Atoms)
  • OGG (Vorbis Comments)
  • MP4 (ISO BMFF / iTunes Atoms)

Unlike any solution before it, DA1metaMetaData follows two core principles:

  1. The neverBlank Principle: Critical metadata fields are never left empty or unsupported across formats.
  2. Creator-Certified Verification: Each metadata transformation is verified and blockchain-anchored to prevent silent corruption.
