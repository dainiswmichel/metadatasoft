The Broadcast Monetization Crisis

The Broadcast Beat investigation reveals several critical ways that metadata issues harm broadcasters' revenue potential:

  • Ad targeting failures when demographic and contextual metadata disappears
  • Rights confusion across international and regional distribution
  • Content discovery breakdown in digital catalogs and OTT platforms
  • Syndication obstacles due to incomplete ownership information
  • Analytics gaps that prevent performance-based monetization

These issues are particularly acute in the broadcast industry, where content regularly traverses multiple distribution channels—from traditional airwaves to OTT platforms, social media, syndication partners, and international distributors. With each transition, critical metadata is often lost or corrupted.
