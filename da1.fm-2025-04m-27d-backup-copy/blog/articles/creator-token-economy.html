The Broken Incentive System

The current ecosystem suffers from fundamental misaligned incentives:

  • Honest creators get nothing for maintaining accurate attribution
  • Metadata manipulation often leads to higher visibility and monetization
  • Platforms have little incentive to maintain attribution integrity
  • No value accrues to those who invest in proper metadata

This creates an environment where the ecosystem rewards manipulation, not integrity. The result is widespread bitterness, resentment, and loss of faith in the digital attribution system itself.
