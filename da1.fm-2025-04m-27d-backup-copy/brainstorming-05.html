Music Industry Solutions

1. Metadata Audit Service for Music Libraries

Who Will Buy & Why:

Independent music labels facing royalty collection issues due to missing or incorrect metadata. Labels lose significant revenue when streaming platforms can't match tracks to rights holders due to metadata inconsistencies.

How To Sell It:
  1. Create a simple one-page audit report showing percentage of recoverable royalties for a small sample of their catalog (5-10 tracks)
  2. Offer a paid full audit with tiered pricing based on catalog size ($2,000-10,000)
  3. Include actionable metadata fixes that can be implemented within 30 days

2. Rights Management Organization Integration

Who Will Buy & Why:

Performance Rights Organizations (PROs) and Collective Management Organizations (CMOs) that face significant challenges matching plays/performances to rights holders. These organizations are actively seeking technological solutions to reduce their "black box" of unattributed royalties.

How To Sell It:
  1. Prepare case study on how metadata inconsistencies create unattributed royalty pools
  2. Offer pilot integration project with success-based pricing (fixed fee plus percentage of recovered attributions)
  3. Focus on demonstrating ROI by reducing manual matching work and increasing attribution rates

3. Attribution Recovery Service for Professional Photographers

Who Will Buy & Why:

Professional photographers who have had their images used without attribution, particularly those whose work is regularly licensed to media outlets. Studies show that 85% of news outlets strip image metadata, leading to lost revenue opportunities and attribution for photographers.

How To Sell It:
